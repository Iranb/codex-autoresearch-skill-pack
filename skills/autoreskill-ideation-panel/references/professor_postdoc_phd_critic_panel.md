# Panel Protocol

Each persona writes an isolated pass with:

- evidence ids used
- strongest argument for
- strongest argument against
- three-innovation bundle assessment: whether the idea has at least three mutually necessary innovation points or only one real point plus filler
- storyline assessment: whether the opening tension, hidden cause, method resolution, and proof ladder form one paper
- weakest assumption
- falsifier pilot
- advance/park/kill recommendation

The Researcher then synthesizes a tournament scoreboard.
