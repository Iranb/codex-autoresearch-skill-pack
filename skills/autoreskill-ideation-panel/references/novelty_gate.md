# Novelty Gate

An idea can advance only if:

- it has target prior evidence
- it has novelty risk evidence or absence-confidence record
- it names a baseline
- it names a primary metric
- it has a falsifier pilot
- it states one defensible core scientific contribution
- its causal signature is distinct, merged, or explicitly an ablation of the closest competing idea
- it names the alternative explanation and cheapest discriminating experiment
- shortlisted ideas have four outcome routes and a claim boundary
- shortlisted ideas record unique decision targets, positive cheapest-falsifier
  GPU-hours with evidence basis, reusable invariant refs, reviewer risks,
  validation density, and the deterministic ranking tuple
- the selected primary has a coherent paper storyline with opening tension, hidden cause, method-as-resolution, proof ladder, reviewer risk/defense, and a 5-7 step narrative spine
- under an enforced `cross_dataset_method`, a method candidate is learner-side
  `ALGO|CODE`, predicts all required dataset legs, names one load-bearing
  parameter/scale and transfer assumption, and has a paired falsifier that can
  separate parameterization failure from mechanism failure

If not, park or kill it. A matching causal signature is merged or routed as an
ablation/control, not renamed into another track. Do not promote unsupported
novelty or use a model-generated success probability.
