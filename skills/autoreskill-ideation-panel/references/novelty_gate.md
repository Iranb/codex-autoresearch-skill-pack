# Novelty Gate

An idea can advance only if:

- it has target prior evidence
- it has novelty risk evidence or absence-confidence record
- it names a baseline
- it names a primary metric
- it has a falsifier pilot
- it has at least three mutually necessary innovation points covering problem/protocol/evaluation, method/mechanism, and training/integration/analysis/validation
- it has a coherent paper storyline with opening tension, hidden cause, method-as-resolution, proof ladder, reviewer risk/defense, and narrative spine

If not, park or kill it. Do not promote unsupported novelty.
